Mount Timpanogas

Me and my cousin decided to hike Mount Timpanogas with eighteen other random people at midnight to see the sunrise. Halfway up the mountain (3.5 miles up) at 3 in the morning, I felt a little faint and like I was going to collapse. Unfortunately I collapsed a minute later. Long story short I had to be carried down the mountain by two random guys I had just met, my cousin, and this random girl; and then I had to be taken to the Emergency Room. In the end everything worked out and I met my bestfriends from this experience. Now we spend our weekends going on more adventures.
